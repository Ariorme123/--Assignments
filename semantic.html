Facts About mermaids

Mermaids are mythical creatures, often depicted with the upper body of a woman and the tail of a fish. They appear in folklore and mythology across many cultures, with stories ranging from benevolent protectors of the sea to dangerous tricksters. While there are numerous mermaid sightings and stories, no scientific evidence supports their existence outside of folklore, according to the National Ocean Service.
